Clan Track Royale

Clear insights for Clan Wars & River Races

Clan Track Royale helps clan leaders and members monitor performance during Clan Wars and River Races. View member contributions, compare statistics during matches, and keep your team coordinated with clear, easy-to-read leaderboards. The app communicates with an API to give up-to-date results during competitions.
